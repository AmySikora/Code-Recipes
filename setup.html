🧰 Set Up Your Coding Kitchen

Before we can start cooking up websites, we need to prep your coding kitchen. Don’t worry — no mess, no flames, just a few simple tools to get started!

  1. 1. Create a CodePen Account
    CodePen is your online kitchen. It’s where you’ll write, test, and preview your recipes.
    👉 Sign up for free at CodePen
  2. 2. Explore the Recipe Collection
    I’ve created a collection of coding recipes you can follow step-by-step.
    👉 Visit the Code Recipes Collection
  3. 3. Learn How to Fork a Recipe
    Click the “Fork” button at the top of any recipe to save your own copy. Then you can customize, remix, or just play around.
  4. 4. Save Your Progress
    Each time you fork a pen, it’s saved to your account — kind of like saving a dish to your cookbook!
  5. 5. Test Your Setup
    Try forking your first recipe: the Spicy Button 🌶️. Can you add your own flavor?

Once your kitchen is ready...

🍽️ Start Cooking
